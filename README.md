This project presents a comprehensive analytical study on the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in the Digital Media ecosystem, developed using Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization and supported by structured data sourced from Kaggle. As digital media platforms continue to expand at an exponential rate, the volume of content being created and consumed has grown beyond what traditional manual workflows can efficiently manage. This project addresses that challenge by leveraging AI-driven insights to examine adoption trends, economic impact, workforce transformation, and competitive market dynamics across global industries.

The dataset encompasses a wide range of criteria including AI adoption rate, AI-generated content volume, revenue increase due to AI, human-AI collaboration rate, tool popularity index, AI regulation level, year-wise growth, market share of leading AI companies, regulatory impact sensitivity, and workforce transformation rate. These metrics collectively provide a multi-dimensional view of how AI is reshaping digital media at both the macro and micro levels.

The Power BI dashboard was built using a systematic data analysis workflow, where relevant dimensions such as country, year, industry, regulation status, consumer trust, and job displacement were carefully extracted and visualized. Chart types were selected based on the nature of each data point — including pie charts, stacked area charts, funnel charts, line charts, DAX-powered cards, and slicers — ensuring that insights are both accessible and actionable for decision-makers.
Key findings reveal that AI has significantly contributed to revenue growth and productivity across media sectors while simultaneously triggering workforce transformation and the rise of human-AI collaboration models. The dashboard also maps the competitive landscape of top AI companies and identifies the most widely adopted AI tools influencing content production and distribution.

Beyond analysis, the project offers strategic suggestions for responsible AI integration — emphasizing the need for algorithmic transparency, bias auditing, misinformation detection, and ethical governance to preserve creativity and fairness in automated media environments.
This project serves as a valuable resource for researchers, media professionals, and policymakers seeking data-backed insights into how AI is adopted across countries, its measurable impact on revenue and employment, and the regulatory forces shaping the future of digital media growth.
